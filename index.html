IIMK ECHOES '19

Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode is proud to present, ECHOES'18, the annual cultural splurge full of fun and fiesta to be held from the 2nd-4th February, 2018. ECHOES'17 witnessed a multitude of events intertwined with frolic and mirth in various aspects of the cultural palette comprising music, dance, fashion, art and theatre with participation from colleges nationwide and prizes worth INR 5 lakh. With brand ECHOES growing over the years, it has now reached its pinnacle and epitomizes cultural extravagance in terms of staging and hosting some of the biggest Pro-Nites in South India with live performances by famous artists such as KK, Sona Mahapatra, Agnee, Neeraj Sridhar, Aditi Singh Sharma, Nikhil D'Souza and many more. Campus Princess, the campus round of Miss India, powered by FBB, presented the first ever Campus Princess in South India, which made brand ECHOES win even more accolades, than before. With bigger hopes and better expectations, ECHOES'18 comes to you with the sweetest theme "Colors on Canvas", to fill your beats with colours, in sync with the 21st anniversary of the inception of IIM Kozhikode. The grandeur of ECHOES'18 will be supplemented by the countless nostalgic memoirs, heartwarming sentiments and surprises galore as this year's carte du jour reveals the sizzling array of events planned and scheduled with the implicit promise of "Kreating Memories".
